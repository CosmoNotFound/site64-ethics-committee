About Me

I am Supreme Rizzler Cosmo, the founder of the Site-64 Ethics Committee and its former Chairman. The Ethics Committee is one of the best-established entities within the SCP RP community, ensuring that all operations align with unethical standards. My leadership has helped shape a sigma chain of command and a skibidi ranking structure.
